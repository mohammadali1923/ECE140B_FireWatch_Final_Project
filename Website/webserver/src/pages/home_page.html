Preventing Disaster,
One Fire at a Time

Preventable fires cause billions of dollars property damage each year, not to mention the countless lives that are lost. Our mission is to one day stop fires before they can start. Our team is developing a drone that can survey large unpopulated areas for fires, and act as an early warning system for firefighters and local officials. Join us!
